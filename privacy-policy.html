Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Silicon City Pet Hospital ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the website includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, address, and pet information
  • Appointment Information: Pet medical history, health records, and appointment details
  • Payment Information: Credit card information processed securely through payment gateways
  • Browsing Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website
  • Contact Information: Information you voluntarily provide through contact forms

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the website to:

  • Process your appointments and medical records
  • Send periodic emails regarding your orders or other products/services
  • Fulfill and manage your orders or appointments
  • Generate a personal profile about you so that future visits to our website are tailored to your preferences
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to your inquiries, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities for marketing and promotional purposes

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information is necessary to comply with the law
  • Third-Party Service Providers: To vendors, consultants, and service providers who assist us in operating our website
  • Your Consent: We will share your information when you give explicit consent

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
