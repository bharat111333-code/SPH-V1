Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring your pet's vaccination records, medical history, and any previous medication details. This helps our veterinarians provide better care.

Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes, we accept most major pet insurance providers. We can help you with the paperwork and billing process.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We try to accommodate appointments within 2-3 days. Emergency cases are seen immediately, 24/7.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes, we offer home visit services for elderly pets or those unable to travel. Please call for details and scheduling.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and online payments. We also offer installment plans for major procedures.

What should I do in a pet emergency?

Call us immediately at our emergency line (080) XXXX-XXXX. We're open 24/7 and ready to help your pet.
