Our Comprehensive Services

Providing the best care for your beloved pets

General Check-ups & Vaccinations

Our experienced veterinarians provide comprehensive health examinations for all types of pets. We ensure your pets receive all necessary vaccinations and preventive care to maintain optimal health.

  • Complete physical examination
  • Vaccination schedules
  • Parasite prevention
  • Microchipping

In-House Pathology Lab

State-of-the-art in-house pathology laboratory equipped with advanced diagnostic equipment for accurate and quick results. We perform blood work, urinalysis, and specialized testing for precise diagnosis.

  • Blood work & biochemistry
  • Urinalysis
  • Digital X-rays
  • Ultrasound imaging

Surgical Services

Professional surgical procedures performed by experienced veterinarians using advanced anesthesia protocols and sterile operating rooms. From routine spaying/neutering to complex surgeries.

  • Spaying & neutering
  • Orthopedic surgery
  • Soft tissue surgery
  • Tumor removal

Dental Care

Comprehensive dental services including cleaning, extractions, and oral health treatments. Poor dental health can lead to serious medical conditions - we help prevent that.

  • Professional cleaning
  • Tooth extractions
  • Oral disease treatment
  • Dental radiography

Pet Spa & Grooming

Professional grooming and spa services to keep your pets looking and feeling their best. Our services promote skin and coat health while providing relaxing treatments.

  • Professional bathing
  • Hair cutting & styling
  • Nail trimming
  • Spa treatments

24/7 Emergency Care

Our emergency services are available 24/7 to handle critical situations. Our experienced team is equipped to handle trauma, toxicity, and life-threatening conditions.

  • Emergency triage
  • Intensive care
  • Trauma management
  • Poison/toxin treatment
